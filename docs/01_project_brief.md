# Project Brief: Logistics & Delivery Performance Analysis
### Shohoz Express Ltd.

---

## Company Background

Shohoz Express Ltd. is a fictional Bangladeshi e-commerce/FMCG distribution company that delivers products across five major regions: Dhaka, Chittagong, Sylhet, Khulna, and Rajshahi. The company relies on multiple third-party carriers to fulfill last-mile delivery across these regions.

## Business Problem

Over the past 6 months, management has observed a rise in customer complaints alongside a decline in the On-Time Delivery (OTD) rate. Delivery costs have also been increasing. However, management currently lacks visibility into:

- Which carriers are underperforming
- Which regions/routes experience the most delays
- Whether there is a relationship between delivery delays and rising costs
- Whether delays follow any time-based pattern (specific days of the week or months)

## Core Business Question

**Where and why is on-time delivery performance and cost efficiency declining in our delivery network — and which specific carrier, region, or route should we target to achieve the greatest improvement?**

## Analysis Scope

1. Overall OTD rate trend (month-over-month)
2. Carrier-wise performance comparison
3. Region/route-wise delay pattern analysis
4. Correlation between delivery delay and cost
5. Day-of-week / seasonal delay pattern
6. Delay reason breakdown (root-cause analysis: traffic, weather, vehicle breakdown, wrong address, warehouse delay)

## Expected Deliverables

- Identification of the top underperforming carrier(s) and region(s)
- Targeted, actionable recommendations (e.g., carrier reallocation, route optimization, SLA renegotiation)
- An interactive Excel dashboard for ongoing monitoring of delivery KPIs

## Tools Used

Microsoft Excel — Power Query (data cleaning), Power Pivot / Data Model (relational modeling), PivotTables & Formulas (analysis), Excel Charts & Slicers (dashboard)

## Dataset Note

The raw dataset used in this project is **intentionally messy**, simulating real-world "as received" operational data. This allows the project to demonstrate a complete, realistic data cleaning workflow using Power Query — documented in detail in a separate Data Cleaning Log.
